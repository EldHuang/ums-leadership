    UMS Student Clubs

    Clubs at UMS are your chance to share a passion with other people with the same passion! Whether you're into science, art, crochet, or something totally unique, starting a club is a powerful way to build community, inspire others, and leave your mark on the school.




    Get Started - Creating A New Club

    Establish
    1. Find a few students with the same interests
      • Students are required inside a club!
    2. Find an Advisor
      • Any
      • Make Sure they Agree to supervise
    Google Form
    1. Complete the Google Form
      1. You will be emailed by - Secretary, and within 5 days of a form submition.
      2. You MUST recive this email to continue
    ASB Meeting
    1. Come to the next after you have recived your email
    2. You are required to PRESENT to the WHOLE board. ASB will vote to approve your club.
